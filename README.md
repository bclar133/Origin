# Origin

State of Origin browser game project, based on lessons from NRL Invincible.

## Starting Idea

Build a fast rugby league game around Queensland v New South Wales selection, tactics, pressure moments, and a three-game Origin series.

## First Playable Rules

- Choose Queensland or New South Wales.
- Spin a random Origin-year state team and pick one player into a valid XIII position.
- The opposition secretly selects a matching player for the same position.
- Pick a strategy and goal kicker before the series starts.
- Play a three-game Origin series at Suncorp Stadium, Accor Stadium, or the MCG.
- Live games show tries, injuries, half-time notes, and full-time commentary.
- Between Games 1 and 2, and Games 2 and 3, up to two form changes are allowed.
- Injured players must be replaced for the rest of the series.
- The aim is to win the series without conceding a point.
- Man of the Match is awarded each game, with a Wally Lewis Medal for the best player of the series.
